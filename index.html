Recipe Specifics

  • 1 gallong water
  • 25 grams tea or 8 tea bags
  • 225 grams sugar
  • 1/2 cup previous batch of kombucha
  • SCOBY

Direction

  • Make Condensed Sweet Tea
  • Combine Cold Water and Tea in Jar
    • Check water temperature
    • Wait if still hot
  • Add SCOBY and Cover

Fermentation

  • Temperature
    • 70 to 85°F
    • Ideal: 75°F
  • Location
    • Out of direct sunlight
    • Consistent temperatures
  • Time
    • 1 to 4 weeks
    • Taste everyday after first week

Too sweet?

  • Continue Fermentation
  • Check Daily

 

Too Tart?

  • Add sugar to bottle or 2nd fermentation
  • Brew shorter next batch

2nd Fermentation

  • Carbonation?
  • 48 hours to 4 days
